Culture Is Something You Create

Ever since I was a kid, I knew that if anything else, I wanted to be different. I didn't like wearing what everyone else was wearing. I didn't like using the same type of structure that most essay's abide by, and I never bought anything just because somebody else had it. I realized that I am the type of person who wants to create a trend rather then follow it. Eventually, I realized that through music, art, and fashion I could start a trend that only I would be proud of.
I aspire to create things that thousands of people stay up all night camped outside for. I want to see my shirt on someone's back and strike up a conversation, without them being aware of who I am, that way I can get critical feedback to implement in a future design. The world is full of individuals
